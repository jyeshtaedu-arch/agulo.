Origin

Born from a quiet
realization.

Agulo was born from a quiet realization: some of India's greatest strengths were never lost — they were simply neglected. Long before industrial food systems reshaped agriculture, the jackfruit tree stood as one of the subcontinent's most generous providers. In village backyards, temple courtyards, and along monsoon-fed fields, this towering tree offered abundance without demand.

"Its fruit fed families through seasons of scarcity, its seeds roasted and ground into nourishing meals — a resilient famine buffer that thrived when other crops failed. The memory of jackfruit never disappeared; it remained embedded in kitchens, rituals, and regional cuisines, waiting to be rediscovered not as nostalgia, but as strategy."

Agulo exists to translate that quiet strength into structured, scalable food formats — from crisp papadums to nutrient-dense dosa mixes and functional blends — preserving cultural continuity while applying contemporary nutritional discipline.

The Tree

Abundance built into
its very architecture.

The jackfruit tree carries a presence that feels almost mythic in scale. It grows massive, steady, unhurried — bearing fruit directly from trunk and branch, as though abundance is built into its very architecture. Each fruit contains layered nourishment: fiber-rich flesh, dense seeds packed with micronutrients, and natural compounds that support digestion and satiety.

It feeds without exhausting the land. It shades, stabilizes soil, and produces for decades. Few trees offer such volume, versatility, and resilience in a single organism. Agulo honors this legacy through renewal — restoring jackfruit to its rightful place in the modern Indian diet.

Our Philosophy

A steady body is built by
what is on the plate.

Every Agulo decision flows from six principles. These are not brand values written in a workshop. They are the constraints we put on ourselves before we put anything in a bag.

01

Jackfruit first,
always

The jackfruit tree is the founding intelligence of Agulo — nutritionally, ecologically, and culturally. Every product line references it directly or in its formulation logic.

02

Low GI
by formulation, not claim

Glycemic index is the first constraint on every formulation. If a preparation raises blood sugar sharply, it does not leave our kitchen under any circumstance.

03

Ancient grains
before modern flour

Ragi, jowar, bajra, kodo millet — not because of trend, but nutritional conviction. These grains sustained communities for centuries before refined flour arrived.

04

No sugar,
no aliases

Maltodextrin, dextrose, corn syrup, fructose by any name — none of it. We read our own ingredient lists like someone who needs to. Because some of our customers do.

05

Fermentation
as transformation

Traditional fermentation lowers glycemic load, improves mineral availability, and makes meals easier on the gut. We preserve this process instead of shortcutting it.

06

Flavour
earns its place

A diabetic-conscious meal that nobody wants to eat has failed. Whole grains, proper fermentation, and honest spices create flavour richer than what they replace.

Sourcing

The tree is the first decision.
Everything follows.

We source jackfruit directly from smallholder growers in Kerala, Karnataka, and Tamil Nadu. For our millet range, we source heirloom varieties from the Karnataka highlands and Telangana. We mill in small batches, stone-ground, to order.

Single-origin jackfruit

Every jackfruit product traces to named orchard groups in Kerala and Karnataka. We know the trees, the harvest season, and the families who tend them.

Stone-milled, small batch

Milled in small quantities to order, at low temperature. Stone grinding preserves bran, germ, and nutrients that roller milling destroys.

Traditional fermentation

Our fermented mixes use live wild fermentation starters — not citric acid, not commercial yeast. The sourness is real. The microbial activity is real.

GI-tested, batch-certified

GI testing is performed on each formulation, not estimated from ingredient tables. Results are published openly and updated with every reformulation.

Integrity

We are accountable to people
who cannot afford to be misled.

I

Every product is GI-tested, not GI-estimated

GI claims are not interpolated from ingredient data. Each Agulo formulation is tested in certified independent conditions and the result is published openly. If a batch reformulation changes the GI by more than five points, the product is retested before relaunch.

II

No refined flour — not even as a filler or binder

Many products advertised as jackfruit-based contain significant proportions of refined wheat flour as binder or cost reducer. We use whole grain alternatives for every function. Read our ingredient list — it tells the complete story.

III

Our sourcing is named, not vague

"Sourced from traditional farms" costs nothing and means nothing. We name the districts, the varieties, and where possible the farming groups we work with. The supply chain is available on request.

IV

The price reflects what it costs to do this honestly

Heirloom jackfruit varieties carry a fair premium. Stone milling is slower. Small batches don't benefit from scale economies. We explain these costs clearly — because the person managing blood sugar deserves to understand what they are paying for.
